Tips

IT'S WHAT YOU DRINK AND HOW YOU DRINK IT.


PREPARE EARLY.

The game before the pre-game.

Set yourself up for success by eating a hearty meal. And yes, go for ALL of the carbs. If you’re already gonna use a cheat day, make it count!

PACE YOURSELF.

Ride the wave, not the ambulance.

Once you've found your optimal buzz, stay there! Drinking games can be fun, but don’t be that guy who doesn't make it out of the pre-game.

TAKE CHARGE.

BYOB: be your own bartender.

Look after your drink so that:

(1) you know what to get next time

(2) you CAN have a next time

HOMIES FIRST.

"Take care of yourselves, and each other."

Check in on your friends frequently. Two brains are greater than one, especially when neither is working at 100% efficiency.

STAY HYDRATED.

Save beer, drink water.

Alcohol is expensive, processed, and hangover-inducing. Water is inexpensive, organic, and hangover-preventing. Be smart. Be a hydro homie.

BE IN CONTROL.

Keep the party at the party.

Don't let one night of fun ruin the rest of your life. Ubers will always be cheaper than hospital bills or bail bonds.
